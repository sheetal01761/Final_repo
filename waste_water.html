Introduction :-

Waste water treatment is a critical issue for any city and country. In our city we see that about a gallon of waste water is merging in river, well and other water resources. It is a very big challenge to convert the waste water into pure and usable one. To overcome this challenge, we have come up with a solution which would be helpful in converting this waste water into pure water and thus resolving this issue to a major extent. There are many cities which are suffering from sewage waste water management problems. People are facing problem of sewage waste water management which causes littering and accumulation of waste water in the fresh water bodies. With a rapid increase in the growth of towns to metro cities, the administration in constant conflicts to manage the accumulation as well as the overflow of sewage water in lakes ,rivers ,on roads etc .Due to this, we are facing so many problems in our day to day life.

APPLICATIONS: :-

IRRIGATION:-

Use of sewage water for irrigation not only improves the growth rate of plants but also reduces the cost of chemical fertilizers. Sewage water should be treated prior to its reuse for agriculture in order to reduce the risks of harmful effects on human and animal health.

INDUSTRY:-

The treated water can be effectively used in industries for fabricating, processing, washing, diluting, cooling, or transporting a product along with use in the boilers of various industries. This water can also be used in the smelting factories, petroleum refineries and industries producing chemical products.

ANIMALS:-

For drinking purpose, animals can use this water.

HOUSES:-

This treated water can be used in garden irrigation purposes like watering of the plants, trees etc at houses.

WORKING

This water purification process will be implemented at the place where sewage waste water gathers. Very first the dirty water will pass through density stainless steel mesh. It will filter the dust which is coming with water. By using the sensors, we see the dirty water containing Turbidity, pH , Density , TDS , and Temperature, Water Dissolved Oxygen etc in the display. Then the water will pass through PP cotton, a type of cotton roll which will stop the dust and powder particles. After passing through chemical chambers, the water will gather in container. The pH, TDS, Turbidity, and Temperature of purified water will be shown in LCD display. Then, the water will be entering in boiler, where it will be boiled in Eco-friendly boiler. This boiler will run through solar plate. We can use this pure water in agriculture, for animals and industries, for household purposes etc.
